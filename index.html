About Me

Khaosara is a third-year design student with a deep passion for pushing the boundaries of user experience (UX) design. Driven by her inquisitive nature, she seeks to fully understand users by exploring both the internal and external factors that influence their decision-making. Through this empathetic approach, Khaosara aims to uncover users' core needs and craft experiences that not only achieve practical objectives but also evoke meaningful emotions. By deconstructing systems and interactions, she develops innovative, relevant, and thoughtful solutions that resonate deeply. She welcomes new challenges and brings fresh perspectives to push boundaries, striving to achieve meaningful milestones and experiences.

Beyond her UX pursuits, Khaosara explores traditional art forms such as painting, sketching, and clay-making, embracing creativity in all its dimensions. She also enjoys cooking and spending time in nature, activities that inspire her holistic approach to design.
